Services

Dental Care Solutions

Orthodontics

Orthodontics focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of malpositioned teeth and jaws. Orthodontists use braces, aligners, and other orthodontic appliances to straighten teeth, correct bite issues, and improve dental alignment

Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery

Oral and maxillofacial surgery involves the diagnosis and surgical treatments and defects of the mouth, jaws and face.Oral surgeons perform procedures such as dental implants, jaw surgery and treatment for oral cancers and traumatic injuries.

Periodontology

Periodontology focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases of the supporting structures of the teeth, including the gums (gingiva), periodontal ligaments, and alveolar bone. Periodontists treat gum disease, perform gum grafts, and place dental implants.

Pediatric Dentistry

It provide specializes in dental care for infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatric dentists focus on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of dental issues specific to children, such as tooth decay, developmental abnormalities, and early orthodontic problems.

Endodontics

Endodontics focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries of the dental pulp and root canal system. Endodontists perform procedures such as root canal therapy, endodontic retreatment, and treatment for dental trauma.

Oral Medicine and Radiology

Oral medicine and radiology involve the diagnosis and management of oral diseases, oral manifestations of systemic diseases, and interpretation of dental radiographs (X-rays) for diagnostic purposes.
